Welcome To STAR MAGNET

What if your child’s greatest strength isn’t just talent — but character? Is your child ready to face life with courage, empathy, and confidence? Are your kids growing up smart... but not always strong in values?
No worries — Star Magnet is here to guide the way--

Star Magnet is a transformative life coaching program crafted specifically for children, with a strong focus on character development. Designed and conducted by the passionate and experienced team at Step Up Foundation, the program aims to nurture the inner strengths of young minds and empower them to become confident, responsible, and compassionate individuals. At the heart of Star Magnet lies a holistic approach to personal growth. The program integrates engaging activities, practical life lessons, and guided reflections to help children cultivate core values such as honesty, empathy, resilience, respect, and self-discipline. Recognizing that character shapes destiny, the curriculum is built to inspire lasting change from within. The Step Up Foundation’s team—comprising skilled educators, child psychologists, and certified life coaches—brings years of experience and deep commitment to every session. Their hands-on and empathetic approach ensures each child is seen, heard, and supported throughout the journey. Through interactive workshops, real-life role play, goal-setting exercises, and consistent mentorship
